# Accessory Ecosystem Specification (br_accessories)

## 1. Overview
This document details the first-party accessory ecosystem for the Airport Business Smartphone. Each accessory is designed with the same philosophy as the phone itself: a focus on professional use cases, premium materials, and seamless integration. The ecosystem is divided into four main categories: Protection, Power, Audio, and Productivity.

## 2. Protection: Executive Cases

### 2.1 The Boardroom Folio
- **Concept**: A sophisticated and functional folio case for the executive on the move.
- **Materials**: Full-grain leather exterior, soft Alcantara interior lining, and a slim polycarbonate frame.
- **Features**:
  - **Card Slots**: Two discreet slots for business cards or a credit card.
  - **Magnetic Closure**: A secure magnetic clasp that also automatically wakes the phone when opened and puts it to sleep when closed.
  - **Kickstand Mode**: The folio cover can be folded into a stable kickstand, perfect for video conferencing or watching presentations.
  - **Pen Holder**: An integrated, slim loop to securely hold the Airport Pen.

### 2.2 The Stealth Guard
- **Concept**: A minimalist case providing maximum protection with minimal bulk.
- **Material**: 600D Aramid Fiber, the same lightweight, high-strength material used in aerospace and body armor.
- **Features**:
  - **Ultra-Slim Profile**: Adds less than 1.5mm of thickness to the phone.
  - **Superior Grip**: A fine, non-slip texture provides a secure in-hand feel.
  - **Full Protection**: A raised bezel protects the screen, and a raised camera ring protects the lenses.

## 3. Power & Charging

### 3.1 HyperCharge 120W GaN Trio Charger
- **Concept**: A single, compact charger for all your devices.
- **Technology**: Gallium Nitride (GaN) technology allows for a much smaller and more efficient design than traditional chargers.
- **Ports**: 2x USB-C PD 3.1 ports (up to 120W) and 1x USB-A port (up to 30W).
- **Intelligent Power Distribution**: Automatically allocates power based on the connected devices. It can fast-charge the Airport phone and a laptop simultaneously.

### 3.2 The Landing Pad
- **Concept**: A sleek, multi-device wireless charging mat for your desk or nightstand.
- **Materials**: Machined aluminum base with a premium Alcantara charging surface.
- **Features**:
  - **Triple Charging Zone**: A dedicated spot for the Airport phone (supporting 100W HyperCharge), a 15W spot for the FocusBuds Pro, and a 5W spot for a smartwatch.
  - **Smart Charging**: Communicates with Airport devices to optimize charging speed and manage thermals.

### 3.3 The Power Reserve
- **Concept**: A slim, portable power bank that supports the full HyperCharge speed.
- **Capacity**: 10,000 mAh.
- **I/O**: 1x USB-C port that supports 120W Power Delivery input and output.
- **Design**: Slim enough to fit in a jacket pocket, with an aluminum casing.

## 4. Audio for Business

### 4.1 FocusBuds Pro (Wireless Earbuds)
- **Concept**: True wireless earbuds designed for crystal-clear communication in noisy environments.
- **Key Feature: Office Aware ANC**: Advanced Active Noise Cancellation with a unique transparency mode that uses AI to specifically filter out common office background noise (like HVAC and distant chatter) while keeping nearby human voices clear.
- **Microphones**: A triple-mic array in each earbud with AI-powered noise reduction to ensure the user's voice is isolated and clear on calls, even in a busy airport or coffee shop.
- **Connectivity**: Seamless multi-point connection, allowing the earbuds to be connected to both the Airport phone and a laptop simultaneously, automatically switching audio sources.

### 4.2 Clarity-Link Earphones (Wired, In-Box)
- **Concept**: A high-quality pair of wired earphones included with every Airport phone, demonstrating a commitment to quality even for standard accessories.
- **Features**:
  - **High-Resolution DAC**: A Digital-to-Analog Converter is built into the USB-C connector for superior, lossless audio quality.
  - **Premium Build**: Braided, tangle-resistant cable and aluminum earphone housings.
  - **Inline Microphone**: A high-quality microphone for reliable, clear calls.

## 5. Productivity & Expansion

### 5.1 The Airport Pen
- **Concept**: An active stylus for precision note-taking, sketching, and system navigation.
- **Features**:
  - **Ultra-Low Latency**: Provides a true pen-on-paper feel.
  - **Pressure & Tilt Sensitivity**: For dynamic line thickness in drawing and writing apps.
  - **AI Button**: A customizable button that can be configured to summon the BeeHive AI for quick actions, such as creating a note from a voice memo or translating highlighted text.
  - **Charging**: Charges wirelessly by magnetically attaching to the side of the Airport phone.

### 5.2 The Command Dock
- **Concept**: A desktop dock that transforms the Airport phone into a full-featured workstation via Desktop Mode.
- **Materials**: Solid, anodized aluminum.
- **Ports**:
  - 1x HDMI 2.1 (for 4K 120Hz display output)
  - 1x Gigabit Ethernet
  - 3x USB-A 3.1
  - 1x USB-C PD input for power
- **Design**: Holds the phone at an ergonomic viewing angle and connects via a single, integrated USB-C cable.
